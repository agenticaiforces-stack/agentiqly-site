Moshe Shimon

Founder & CEO

Moshe is the founder of Agentiqly, an AI agency that builds production-grade software with a fractional team of specialized AI agents operating under a single domain expert.

He built the complete Agentiqly ecosystem from the ground up — a 9-agent AI engineering team powered by OpenClaw orchestration that delivers full-stack platforms in weeks instead of months. His approach eliminates the traditional overhead of hiring cycles, communication gaps, and multi-vendor coordination.

Under his leadership, Agentiqly has delivered SNF IQ (snfiq.io) — a full-stack AI acquisition intelligence platform for skilled nursing facility investors, launched in 10 weeks by a single expert — and TRAX CRM (traxcrm.help), a multi-tenant referral management platform for post-acute care. Both are HIPAA compliant by design.

Moshe owns and operates New York SEO (linkedin.com/in/newyorkseo), a digital growth practice, and holds a portfolio of 20+ domain assets spanning AI, healthcare, and enterprise SaaS verticals under the Agentiqly holding company.
