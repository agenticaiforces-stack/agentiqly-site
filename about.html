The R.F.V.P. Methodology

Everything we build follows the same 4-phase engine:

🔬 Phase R — Research & Discovery

We embed in your workflow before we write a single line of code. Interviews, process mapping, data audit. We need to understand your business better than your own team does.

📐 Phase F — Framework & Design

Architecture documents, agent role definitions, guardrail specifications, tech stack decisions, and cost modeling. Everything is designed on paper before any agent is built.

🧪 Phase V — Validation & Test

Staged rollouts. A/B testing against your current processes. Failure mode analysis. We break things in staging so they don't break in production.

🚀 Phase P — Production & Deploy

Live deployment with monitoring, audit trails, and emergency controls. You get a dashboard, not a black box. And we stay on retainer.
